About Me

My name is Mehdi Nouira, a cybersecurity student who thrives on the exciting challenges of cyberspace. I am focused on solving the hard problems of cybersecurity and I am very much interested in international opportunities where I can not only learn the best global practices, but also be part of the solution to safeguard the cyber world.

Taking part in an international program will definitely enable me to work alongside professionals and acquire new points of view from the global perspectives of cybersecurity issues and solutions.
